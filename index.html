Welcome to Sunnywood

Sunnywood is closed. Thank you for your interest.

Create

We strive to create a better world though lending to the success of others doing great work. We are sincerely interested in the mission of the companies we serve and we are inspired by them.

Design

Our intent is to work creatively and cooperatively designing communication tools essential to any company, but particularly for those furthering the common good through environmental sustainability or local community.

Develop

We build tools for communication: web sites, visual identities, print design, interactive media, and other work, developed with a focus on comprehensive design. We apply the same devotion to our craft whether it is basic graphic design or complex database-driven web applications.

Sustain

We come to this work not as designers, programmers, and production specialists, but as people, passionate about community and environment. We trust that as this work sustains us and our families it extends further to a more sustainable future for all.
